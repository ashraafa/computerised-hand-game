Rock Paper Scissors Lizard Spock

Rock Paper Scissors Lizard Spock is a variation of Rock Paper Scissors.

The game adds the Lizard and Spock so there are fewer ties when you play.

Playing Rules

  1. Player selects a hand
  2. The Game randomly selects a hand
  3. Winner is determined as defined in "Game Rules"
  4. First player to win 2 rounds scores 1 game point
  5. First player to win 3 games is the winner

Game Rules

  • Rock crushes Lizard and Scissors
  • Paper covers Rock and disproves Spock
  • Scissors cuts Paper and decapitates Lizard
  • Lizard poisons Spock and eats Paper.
  • Spock smashes Scissors and vaporizes Rock
